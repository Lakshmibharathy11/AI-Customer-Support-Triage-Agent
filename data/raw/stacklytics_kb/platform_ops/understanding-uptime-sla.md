# Understanding our uptime SLA

## Introduction to Uptime SLA
Stacklytics is committed to providing a reliable and high-availability product-analytics SaaS for engineering teams. Our uptime SLA is designed to ensure that our customers can access our services without interruption. The uptime SLA is measured based on the availability of our API, which can be accessed at https://api.stacklytics.io/v2. Our support team is available to assist with any issues, Monday-Friday, 9am-5pm Pacific Time.

## Uptime SLA Tiers
Our uptime SLA varies by tier, with more comprehensive guarantees for higher-tier customers. The following table summarizes the uptime SLA for each tier:
| Tier | Uptime SLA |
| --- | --- |
| Gold | 99.5% |
| Diamond | 99.9% |
| Platinum | 99.95% |
This means that Platinum customers can expect the highest level of availability, with less than 5 minutes of downtime per month.

## Monitoring and Support
To ensure that our uptime SLA is met, we monitor our services closely and have a robust support system in place. Customers can check the status of our services at https://status.stacklytics.io. If an issue arises, our support team is available to assist, with response times varying by tier, from 8 hours for Gold customers to 1 hour for Platinum customers. In the event of a billing dispute, customers must submit a ticket tagged "billing-dispute" within 14 days of the invoice date.
