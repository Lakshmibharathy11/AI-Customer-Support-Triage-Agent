# Checking platform status

## Introduction to Platform Status
To ensure you're always up-to-date on the current status of our platform, Stacklytics provides a dedicated status page. You can access this page at https://status.stacklytics.io, where you'll find real-time information on any ongoing issues or maintenance. Our status page is regularly updated to reflect the current state of our services, including any disruptions or scheduled maintenance. This is especially important for our Platinum tier customers, who are guaranteed an uptime SLA of 99.95%.

## Checking for Issues
If you're experiencing issues with the Stacklytics platform, such as receiving error codes like 401 (invalid API key) or 429 (rate limit exceeded, also known as RATE_LIMIT_EXCEEDED), you should first check the status page for any known problems. Our support team works diligently to resolve issues as quickly as possible, with response times varying by tier: 1 hour for Platinum, 4 hours for Diamond, and 8 hours for Gold. If you're unable to find any information on the status page, you can submit a support ticket for further assistance.

## Uptime and Reliability
At Stacklytics, we're committed to providing a reliable and high-availability platform for all our customers. Our uptime SLA varies by tier, with Platinum customers receiving a 99.95% guarantee, Diamond customers receiving 99.9%, and Gold customers receiving 99.5%. If you have any concerns about platform status or would like more information on our uptime guarantees, you can review our support documentation or reach out to our support team for assistance.
