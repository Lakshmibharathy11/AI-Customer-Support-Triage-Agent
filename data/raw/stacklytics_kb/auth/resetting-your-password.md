# Resetting your password

## Introduction to Password Reset
If you're having trouble accessing your Stacklytics account, you may need to reset your password. This process is straightforward and can be completed in a few steps. As a reminder, your Stacklytics account is tied to your company's product-analytics SaaS, which is used by your engineering team to track events and gain valuable insights. Whether you're on the Gold, Diamond, or Platinum tier, resetting your password will not affect your current plan or features.

## Resetting Your Password
To reset your password, navigate to the Stacklytics login page and click on the "Forgot Password" link. Enter your email address associated with your Stacklytics account, and you will receive an email with a password reset link. If you encounter any issues during this process, you can reach out to our support team, who will respond within the timeframe designated by your tier: 8 hours for Gold, 4 hours for Diamond, and 1 hour for Platinum. Keep in mind that our support hours are Monday-Friday, 9am-5pm Pacific Time.

## Troubleshooting Common Issues
If you're experiencing issues with your API key after resetting your password, you may receive a 401 error, indicating an invalid API key. In this case, you'll need to update your API key in your application, using the API base URL https://api.stacklytics.io/v2. Additionally, be mindful of rate limits to avoid receiving a 429 error, which indicates that the rate limit has been exceeded. If you have any further questions or concerns, you can check our status page at https://status.stacklytics.io for any ongoing issues or outages.
