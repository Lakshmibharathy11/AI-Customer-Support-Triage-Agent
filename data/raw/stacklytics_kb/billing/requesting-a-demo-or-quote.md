# Requesting a demo or quote

## Introduction to Stacklytics
Stacklytics is a B2B product-analytics SaaS designed for engineering teams, offering three customer tiers: Gold, Diamond, and Platinum. Each tier provides a unique set of features and support options, including standard support response times of 8 hours for Gold, 4 hours for Diamond, and 1 hour for Platinum. The Platinum tier also includes a dedicated Customer Success Manager (CSM) and Single Sign-On (SSO) with SAML. To get started with Stacklytics, you can explore our features and pricing on our website.

## Requesting a Demo
If you're interested in learning more about Stacklytics and how it can benefit your engineering team, you can request a demo. During the demo, our team will walk you through the features and capabilities of our product, including our API and SDKs for JavaScript, Python, and Go. You'll also have the opportunity to ask questions and discuss your specific use case. To request a demo, simply submit a ticket through our support portal, and we'll be in touch to schedule a time that works for you.

## Obtaining a Quote
For teams that are ready to move forward with Stacklytics, we offer customized quotes based on your specific needs and requirements. Our quotes outline the costs associated with each tier, including the number of seats, events per month, and support options. For example, our Gold tier includes 1 seat and 1M events per month, while our Platinum tier offers unlimited seats and events. To obtain a quote, please submit a ticket through our support portal, and we'll work with you to create a customized quote that meets your needs.
