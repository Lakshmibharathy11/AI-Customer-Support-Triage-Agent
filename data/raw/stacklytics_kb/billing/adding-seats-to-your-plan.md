# Adding seats to your plan

## Introduction to Adding Seats
Adding seats to your plan allows you to expand access to Stacklytics for your growing engineering team. The number of seats available to you depends on your current tier: Gold includes 1 seat, Diamond includes up to 10 seats, and Platinum offers unlimited seats. If you're on the Gold tier and need more seats, you'll need to upgrade to Diamond or Platinum. You can check your current tier and seat count by logging into your Stacklytics account.

## Tier Comparison for Seats and Events
The following table compares the seat and event limits across our tiers:
| Tier | Seats | Events per Month |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Gold | 1 | 1M |
| Diamond | up to 10 | 10M |
| Platinum | unlimited | unlimited |
This table highlights the key differences in seat and event capacity between our tiers, helping you decide which tier best fits your team's needs.

## Upgrading Your Plan
To add more seats, you may need to upgrade your plan from Gold to Diamond or Platinum. Platinum tier offers dedicated support, SSO/SAML, and a 99.95% uptime SLA, making it ideal for large teams. When upgrading, keep in mind that our billing terms change: Diamond and Platinum tiers are invoiced, while Gold is auto-charged. If you have any questions about upgrading or the billing process, you can submit a ticket to our support team, available Monday-Friday, 9am-5pm Pacific Time.
