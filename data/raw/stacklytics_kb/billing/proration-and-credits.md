# Proration and credits explained

## Introduction to Proration and Credits
Proration and credits are essential concepts in managing your Stacklytics subscription. When you downgrade your plan, you are eligible for prorated credits, which can be used towards future invoices. This policy applies to all tiers, including Gold, Diamond, and Platinum. It's essential to note that prorated credits are calculated based on the number of days remaining in your billing cycle.

## How Proration Works
When you downgrade your plan, the proration calculation is based on the difference in cost between your old and new plans. For example, if you downgrade from a Diamond plan to a Gold plan, you will receive a prorated credit for the remaining days in your billing cycle. The credit amount will be calculated based on the number of days remaining and the cost difference between the two plans. You can find more information about your billing cycle and invoices on the 1st of each month, when invoices are issued.

## Refund and Credit Policies
In addition to prorated credits, Stacklytics also offers a 30-day money-back guarantee on annual plans. If you are not satisfied with your subscription, you can request a refund within 30 days of your initial purchase. Refunds and credits are subject to our standard billing policies, which can be found on our website. If you have any disputes or questions about your invoice, please submit a ticket tagged "billing-dispute" within 14 days of the invoice date, and our support team will review your case promptly, in accordance with our support response times, which vary by tier, from 1 hour for Platinum to 8 hours for Gold.
