# Platinum plan overview

## Introduction to Platinum Plan
The Platinum plan is the top tier offered by Stacklytics, designed to provide the highest level of service and support for engineering teams. This plan includes unlimited seats, unlimited events, and Single Sign-On (SSO) with SAML, ensuring seamless integration and scalability. With a dedicated Customer Success Manager (CSM), Platinum customers receive personalized support and guidance. The Platinum plan also features a 1-hour support response time, with escalations reviewed within 2 business hours.

## Key Features and Benefits
One of the key benefits of the Platinum plan is the high level of support and reliability it offers. With a 99.95% uptime SLA, Platinum customers can trust that their service will be available when they need it. In contrast to the Gold and Diamond plans, which have lower uptime SLAs of 99.5% and 99.9% respectively, the Platinum plan provides the highest level of reliability. The following table summarizes the key features of each plan:
| Tier | Seats | Events | Support Response Time | Uptime SLA |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Gold | 1 | 1M/mo | 8 hours | 99.5% |
| Diamond | up to 10 | 10M/mo | 4 hours | 99.9% |
| Platinum | unlimited | unlimited | 1 hour | 99.95% |

## Support and Resources
Platinum customers have access to priority support, with a response time of 1 hour, Monday through Friday, 9am-5pm Pacific Time. In case of common errors such as 401 (invalid API key), 429 (rate limit exceeded), or SDK-1042 (event schema validation failure), customers can refer to the Stacklytics API documentation at https://api.stacklytics.io/v2 or contact their dedicated CSM for assistance. For real-time updates on system status, customers can visit the Stacklytics status page at https://status.stacklytics.io.
