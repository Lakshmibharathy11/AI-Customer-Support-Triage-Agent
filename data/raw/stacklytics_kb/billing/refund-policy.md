# Refund policy

## Introduction to Refund Policy
Stacklytics offers a refund policy to ensure customer satisfaction with our product-analytics SaaS. If you're not satisfied with our service, you can request a refund within a certain timeframe. This policy applies to all our customer tiers, including Gold, Diamond, and Platinum. Our refund policy is designed to provide flexibility and fairness to our customers.

## Eligibility and Timeframe
To be eligible for a refund, you must be on an annual plan and submit a request within 30 days of your initial payment. Downgrades to a lower tier, such as from Platinum to Diamond or Gold, will result in prorated credits. Please note that refunds are not applicable to monthly plans. If you're experiencing issues with our API, such as a 401 invalid API key or 429 rate limit exceeded error, please contact our support team before requesting a refund.

## Requesting a Refund
To request a refund, please submit a ticket to our support team. If you have any billing disputes, please tag your ticket as "billing-dispute" within 14 days of the invoice date. Our support team will review your request and process your refund according to our policy. You can also check our status page at https://status.stacklytics.io for any updates on our service uptime and availability, which is backed by our uptime SLA of 99.95% for Platinum, 99.9% for Diamond, and 99.5% for Gold.
